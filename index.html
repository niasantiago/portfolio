About Me
I am a Full Stack Software Developer

With extensive experience in computer science and a history of engaging with diverse individuals, my passion for ideation and effective communication has flourished. My fascination with the technology industry began at an early age, leading me to pursue a journey with Girls Who Code at age 18. Since then, my ardor for technology has grown exponentially, as programming has enabled me to channel my love for innovation, troubleshoot complex challenges, and build practical websites with precision and care. These proficiencies have allowed me to allocate my time productively and meet deadlines effectively as a software engineer. My vocation has transcended a professional pursuit and has become a fervent passion. As a developer, my objective is to create an inclusive and accessible space for individuals within my community and around the globe. My unwavering passion for this cause has persisted for years, and I am confident that I can bring immense value to any team and company in the future.
